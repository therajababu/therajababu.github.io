Biography

Raja Babu is a Software Engineer in the FinTech Company, and a resident of Deoghar, Jharkhand. He graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur with a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) Degree. He has been Researcher on Machine Learning at Tomsk Polytechnic University (TPU), Russia. He is a former Student Coordinator of IIT Kanpur's Unnat Bharat Abhiyan - A Flagship program of the Ministry of Education. He takes an active interest in working towards social causes.
